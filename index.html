Our Story

Your Trusted Mobile Partner

Mobile Gallery was founded in 2015 with a simple idea – give every customer in Bihar a reliable place to buy genuine phones from all major brands, at honest prices, without any compromise.

Today we're proud to have served over 10,000 happy customers who keep coming back. Our team of experienced mobile experts helps you choose the best phone for your budget and needs.
